introduction :

This app is use to analysis the crab data.

This data frame contains the following columns:

"sp", which means species--"B" or "O" for blue or orange.

"sex", as it says, the gender

"FL",which means frontal lobe size (mm)

"RW",which means rear width (mm)

"CL",which means carapace length (mm)

"CW",which means carapace width (mm)

"BD",which means body depth (mm)

In this app, you can easily see the table made by sex and species of crabs.

further more, you can observe the information and distribution of the data accurately and clearly with interactive plots.
